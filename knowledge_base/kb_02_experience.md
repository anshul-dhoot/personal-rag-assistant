# KB-02: Years of Experience

**Q: How many years of experience do you have?**

I have 17 years of engineering experience, spanning individual contribution, tech leadership, and engineering management.

Here's how it breaks down:

**2009–2016 (7 years) — Engineer to Principal Engineer**
Infosys (client: Cisco), HSBC, and Nisum (client: GAP). Hands-on Java, Spring Boot, test automation, and full-stack delivery. Built core skills in backend engineering, API development, and QA automation across telecom, banking, and retail domains.

**2016–2020 (4 years) — Tech Lead**
Principal Financial Group and Citibank. Led teams of 6–8 engineers. Delivered insurance platform features, Flink-based near real-time pipelines, ETF lifecycle APIs, and regulatory reporting modules. First exposure to leading delivery end-to-end.

**2020–Present (5 years) — Engineering Manager, Deutsche Bank**
Started as AVP Tech Lead, promoted to VP Engineering Manager in 2024. Built and scaled a GCP data platform team from 8 to 16+ engineers. Led cloud migration, deployed AI in production, and operated at CIO-level stakeholder visibility.

**Domain depth:** 17 years in financial services — banking, insurance, fintech, and retail. The last 5 years focused specifically on data platforms, cloud-native architecture, and AI/GenAI adoption at enterprise scale.
