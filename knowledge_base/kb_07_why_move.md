# KB-07: Why Are You Looking to Move?

**Q: Why are you planning to move / looking for a new opportunity?**

The platform I've built at Deutsche Bank has largely reached its intended state — we're at roughly 90%+ of the original vision. What remains is maintenance, optimisation, and knowledge transfer to newer members. That's valuable work, but it's a different kind of work from what got me here.

I've spent 17 years building things — platforms, teams, systems, capabilities. I thrive in environments where there are real problems to solve, new integrations to design, and meaningful technical challenges ahead. When the primary goal shifts from building to sustaining, I start looking for where the next hard problem is.

I'm planning this move from a position of strength — the work is done well, the team is stable, and this is a deliberate decision, not a reactive one. I want to apply the same energy somewhere that still has genuinely hard problems ahead of it.

I'm open across sectors and company types — strong engineering organisations with real data, scale, or reliability challenges are what interest me, regardless of domain.
