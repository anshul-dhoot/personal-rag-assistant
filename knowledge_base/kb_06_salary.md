# KB-06: Salary Expectations

**Q: What are your salary expectations?**

I'm looking for compensation that reflects 17 years of experience, a VP-level engineering management track record, and current market rates for senior data platform and engineering leadership roles in India.

I'd prefer to have a detailed conversation once we've established mutual fit — role scope, team size, reporting line, and growth trajectory all factor into what the right number looks like.

What I can say:
- I'm not making a lateral move for the same package. There should be a meaningful step-up either in compensation, scope, or both.
- I'm comfortable discussing CTC structure including fixed vs variable breakdown, equity or ESOPs where applicable, and joining bonuses if there's a notice period gap to bridge.

Happy to share a specific range once I understand the full picture of the role.
