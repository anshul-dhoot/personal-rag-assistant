# KB-08: IC vs Engineering Manager — What Are You Open To?

**Q: Are you open to IC roles or only Engineering Management?**

I'm genuinely open to both — IC and EM — and I mean that without qualification.

As an EM, I bring team-building, delivery leadership, hiring, and the ability to grow engineers into leaders. As an IC — Staff or Principal Engineer — I bring architectural depth, cross-team technical influence, and the ability to work at the intersection of system design and business outcomes.

What matters to me is the scope and impact of the role, not the label. A Staff Engineer role with real architectural ownership is as interesting to me as an EM role with genuine people and delivery responsibility. I'm not attached to a title — I'm attached to doing meaningful work at the right level of seniority.
