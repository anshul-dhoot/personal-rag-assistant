# KB-04: Location & Work Mode

**Q: Where are you based and what's your work mode preference?**

I'm based in Pune, Maharashtra — have been for the past 15 years, though I'm originally from Ajmer, Rajasthan. I'm open to any location across India and any work mode — onsite, hybrid, or remote. Wherever the right opportunity is, I'm willing to go.

---

**Q: You've spent 15 years in Pune — why are you open to moving?**

Pune has been my base for 15 years, but I've never put down roots in a way that makes leaving difficult. I've moved across 5 different places within Pune itself — I don't own property here, and my family is adaptable. Ajmer is home originally, so I've always had a sense that where I live is a practical decision, not an emotional one.

More importantly, I'm at a stage in my career where the *opportunity* matters more than the *postcode*. If the right role is in Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, or anywhere else in India, I'm genuinely willing to move. I'd rather be somewhere doing meaningful work than comfortable in a familiar city doing less.
