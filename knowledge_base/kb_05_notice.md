# KB-05: Notice Period

**Q: What is your notice period?**

My current notice period at Deutsche Bank is 3 months, which is standard for VP-level roles in large banks.

That said, I'm open to discussing early release depending on how the transition is managed — this is always a conversation worth having once there's a concrete offer and timeline on the table.

If you're working to a specific joining date, let's align on it early so we can plan accordingly.
